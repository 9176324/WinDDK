[AC97SMPL.Services], [AC97SMPL.NTX86.Services]
The Services section contains one or more AddService directives, specifying the services installed by the driver, how and when are they loaded and possible dependencies they might have on other underlying drivers or services.

Entries and values

AddService=ServiceName,[flags],service-install-section...

ServiceName = AC97SMPL

Flags = 0x00000002 - this flag marks the named service as the PnP function driver for the particular device being installed by this INF file.

service-install-section = [AC97SMPL_Service_Inst]

See also the Glossary entry for [DDInstall.Services].
